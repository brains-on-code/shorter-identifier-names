Data protection and privacy are very important to us and the University of Heidelberg. During the experiment some data will be collected, sent to our severs and stored. It will be used for statistic analysis and will be published in an aggregated form.

The raw data will be published with all personal data removed and can be found on Github

What data is being collected?

During the experiment you will be presented with a questionnaire. There are open, visible questions, but at one point there will be a protocol in the background. Your actions are recorded to create an image of your behavior.

What will not be collected?

No data is being collected, that will allow us to identify you as an individual, such as name, ip, facebook-cookie, etc.

At the end of the study you will be asked to leave an email adress, but doing so is optional. It will be stored without any connection to the collected data.
